Ingredients of Choco Lava Brownie.....

(1)   7 tbsp butter

(4)   ⅔ cup Cadbury Cocoa Powder
(5)   1¼ tsp baking powder

(7)   1 cup powdered sugar
(8)   ½ tsp vanilla extract
(9)   ½ cup plain curd
(10)  2 bars whole Cadbury Milk
(11)  ½ cup semi-sweet chocolate chips (optional)
